ZTV Memory Recall

⚡ ZTV Memory Recall is a very challenging, colourful memory game. You watch a quick cue sequence, then tap the on-screen buttons in the exact same order. It looks simple until the levels start stacking.

This game uses colour, working memory, and careful reading of the on-screen instructions to train your brain. Not only do you have to remember the pattern, you have to understand exactly how to submit your answer to pass the level.

Key features

  • 🎯 300 levels that ramp up gradually from chill to brutal.
  • 📊 Live HUD showing Level, Accuracy %, and Best Streak.
  • ⚙️ Fast rounds with snappy feedback and no clutter.
  • 🧠 Focus, recall, and attention all working at the same time.
  • 📱 Progress and stats saved on your device - no account needed.

How to play

  1. Tap Start Level to watch the cue sequence.
  2. Tap the coloured buttons to enter the same order.
  3. Tap Submit to lock in your answer.
  4. Nail it to level up. Miss a step and try again.
