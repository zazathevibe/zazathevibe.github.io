Become a tester

Submissions go to zazathevibetarot@gmail.com through the form below (FormSubmit). Add the Google account email that’s on your phone and pick one app or all testing-track apps. There is no automatic “one-click” enroll from a static website — after we read your request, we add that account in Google Play Console; then Google Play emails the official tester invite you tap to install.

Reviewer commitment: after install, open every app you’re enrolled for at least once per day for 14 days straight so sessions, crashes, and telemetry line up for a clear report.

Exact requirements per app — minimum Android version and tester expectations — live on each app’s page. Check there before signing up.

Delivers to zazathevibetarot@gmail.com via FormSubmit.
