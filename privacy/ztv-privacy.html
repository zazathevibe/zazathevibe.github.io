Advertising (Google AdMob)

Some apps use Google AdMob. When ads are shown, Google may collect:

  • AAID (advertising identifier)
  • Approximate location (coarse, non-GPS)
  • Ad interactions (impressions, views, performance)
  • Crash diagnostics related to ads

All of this data is processed solely by Google. The developer does NOT collect, store, or receive this information.

Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

No Personal Data Collected by Developer
