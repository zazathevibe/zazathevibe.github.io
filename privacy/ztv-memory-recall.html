Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications published under the ZTV / ZaZa The Vibe developer account, including:

  • ZTV Impulse (com.ztv.impulse)
  • ZTV Dream Decoder (tv.ztv.app)
  • ZaZa The Vibe Tarot (com.zazathevibe.tarot)
  • ZTV Memory Recall (com.ztv.memoryrecall)

All apps use Google AdMob for advertising. No personal information is collected, stored, shared, or sold by the developer. Any data handled within the apps is processed solely by Google according to their policies.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The apps integrate the Google Mobile Ads SDK. Google may automatically collect:

  • Device identifiers (such as AAID)
  • Approximate location (coarse, non-GPS)
  • App interactions (ad views, impressions, performance data)
  • Crash diagnostics related to ads

These data points help Google deliver, maintain, and improve advertising and to prevent fraud. The developer does not receive or store this information.

For more details about how Google uses data, refer to: https://policies.google.com/privacy

No Personal Data Collected by Developer

ZTV / ZaZa The Vibe apps do NOT collect:

  • Names, emails, phone numbers, or contact information
  • Photos, media, or files
  • Precise location
  • User-generated content
  • Usage analytics, behavioral analytics, or tracking data
  • Financial or payment information

The apps do not create accounts, do not store data on servers, and do not share information with third parties.

Children’s Policy

These apps are not directed to children under 13. Google AdMob automatically applies families-compliance rules if required.

Data Retention & Deletion

The developer stores no data; therefore, no deletion request is necessary. Any data collected by Google can be managed through Google account settings or device ad settings.
