Minimum Android · what we log

Compatible devices: Android 8.0 (Oreo, API 26) or newer, ARM phone or tablet with Google Play and Play services — same minimum Google exposes for this APK. Older OS versions simply won’t install.

Tester workload: once you accept the invite and install from Play, launch this app at least once each day for 14 days in a row. Regular sessions document progression algorithms and pacing tuning.
