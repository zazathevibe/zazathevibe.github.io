ZTV Dream Decoder

🌙 ZTV Dream Decoder is a modern dream dictionary and angel-number guide in one app. Type full sentences with words and numbers, and the decoder pulls out the symbols and meanings for you.

It’s built for real-world dreams: messy, layered, and full of repeating signs. You get straight-up interpretations instead of vague fluff.

What you can decode

  • 💤 Classic dream symbols - animals, places, emotions, objects.
  • 🔢 Numbers 1-100, angel numbers 111-999, 1000, and 1,000,000.
  • 🧩 Mixed sentences like "snake 777 in dark water with candles".
  • 📚 Clean dictionary-style entries with no duplicate spam.
  • 📝 "All seriousness" summaries that pull everything together.

Why use it

If you keep seeing the same animals, people, or numbers in dreams, ZTV Dream Decoder gives you one place to log it, decode it, and start spotting patterns over time.
